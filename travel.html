Airport

Travelers should fly into San Jose International Airport (SJC). The airport is a 10-minute drive from the Mission.

Transportation

Travelers not renting a car at the airport can use the VTA Airport Flyer. This free shuttle stops at the Metro Light Rail station (with service to downtown San Jose) and the Santa Clara Caltrain station (directly across the street from the Mission).
